Giulia Gartner (@guiligartner) is an outdoor, travel, and commercial photographer, filmmaker, and storyteller from the Dolomites in northern Italy. Her work focuses on capturing wild and rugged landscapes with a vivid color palette and dreamy elements. Her love for photography has spilled over into filmmaking which is one of the main creative outlets she pursues today.
